Tools to Help Achieve Climate Action

Scientists and policymakers use various tools and technologies to fight climate change. Below are some of the most important ones:

  • Solar Panels: Solar energy is one of the fastest-growing renewable energy sources, providing clean power without emissions.
  • Weather Satellites: These are used to track climate patterns and extreme weather events like hurricanes.
  • Carbon Footprint Calculators: These tools help individuals and businesses measure their environmental impact.

Measuring Progress

Global organizations measure progress by tracking greenhouse gas emissions, monitoring temperature changes, and measuring the health of ecosystems. NASA’s Climate Change Data provides real-time data on these metrics.
