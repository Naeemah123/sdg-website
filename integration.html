How Science Relates to Climate Action

In this unit, you will explore the scientific principles behind weather and climate. We’ll learn how climate change is affecting these systems, and how renewable energy and sustainable practices can help.

Key Topics:

  • The Greenhouse Effect: How greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere.
  • Renewable Energy: Solar, wind, and other forms of clean energy.
  • Tracking Climate Change: How scientists measure global temperature changes and their impact on ecosystems.

Class Activity:

As part of this lesson, you'll track daily weather patterns for one month to see how they compare with historical data.
