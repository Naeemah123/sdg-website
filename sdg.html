Understanding Climate Action (SDG 13)

Climate Action aims to combat the effects of climate change by reducing greenhouse gas emissions, switching to renewable energy, and protecting vulnerable ecosystems.

Why It Matters: Rising global temperatures lead to extreme weather events such as hurricanes, floods, and droughts, which threaten communities around the world.
